培训场地地址

Venue Address

成都瑧林MUSTEL木文缇酒店 · 三楼会议中心

MUSTEL Hotel · 3rd Floor Conference Center

开车前往

By Car

1

主要路线选择

Main Route Selection

从市中心（天府广场）出发，沿人民南路南行，转入天府大道，直行至天府三街路口。

From downtown (Tianfu Square), head south on Renmin South Road, turn onto Tianfu Avenue, and go straight to the intersection of Tianfu 3rd Street.

2

驶入天府三街

Enter Tianfu 3rd Street

在天府三街路口右转，沿天府三街向西行驶约500米。

Turn right at the intersection of Tianfu 3rd Street and drive west for about 500 meters.

3

抵达酒店

Arrive at Hotel

酒店位于天府三街南侧，请留意"瑧林MUSTEL木文缇酒店"标识，地下停车场入口在酒店正门右侧。

The hotel is located on the south side of Tianfu 3rd Street. Look for the "MUSTEL Hotel" sign. The underground parking entrance is on the right side of the main entrance.

4

停车信息

Parking Information

酒店提供地下停车场，年会参会车辆可凭会议凭证享受免费停车服务。停车场电梯可直达三楼会议中心。

The hotel provides underground parking. Annual meeting attendees can enjoy free parking with conference credentials. The parking elevator goes directly to the 3rd floor conference center.

开车路线示意图

Driving Route Map

从天府广场到酒店约8.5公里，预计行驶时间25-35分钟

About 8.5km from Tianfu Square to the hotel, estimated driving time 25-35 minutes

温馨提示：早高峰时段（7:30-9:00）天府大道车流量较大，建议提前出发。酒店周边也有多个公共停车场可供选择。

Note: During morning rush hours (7:30-9:00), Tianfu Avenue has heavy traffic. We recommend departing earlier. There are also several public parking lots around the hotel.

地铁前往

By Metro

1

地铁线路选择

Metro Line Selection

乘坐成都地铁1号线，前往科学城方向，在天府三街站下车。

Take Chengdu Metro Line 1 (toward Science City) and get off at Tianfu 3rd Street Station.

2

选择出口

Select Exit

从地铁天府三街站C1或C2出口出站，这两个出口距离酒店最近。

Exit from C1 or C2 exit of Tianfu 3rd Street Station. These exits are closest to the hotel.

3

步行指引

Walking Directions

出站后沿天府三街向西步行约300米（约5分钟），酒店位于道路南侧。

After exiting, walk west along Tianfu 3rd Street for about 300 meters (approx. 5 minutes). The hotel is on the south side of the road.

4

进入酒店

Enter Hotel

从酒店正门进入，前台左侧有专用电梯可直达三楼会议中心。

Enter through the main entrance. There is a dedicated elevator on the left side of the front desk that goes directly to the 3rd floor conference center.

地铁及步行路线图

Metro & Walking Route Map

地铁1号线天府三街站 → 步行5分钟 → 酒店

Metro Line 1 Tianfu 3rd Street Station → 5-minute walk → Hotel

温馨提示：地铁1号线早高峰时段较为拥挤，建议提前规划时间。从火车南站换乘仅需2站即可到达天府三街站。

Note: Metro Line 1 is crowded during morning rush hours. We recommend planning your time accordingly. From South Railway Station, it's only 2 stops to Tianfu 3rd Street Station.
